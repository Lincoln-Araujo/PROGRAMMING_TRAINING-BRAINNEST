Walks

* return to Hobbies

Go out for a walk in the end of the day is something that I love to do.

But, some days a I'm full of energy and do walks a little more audacious.

  • Walking in the city

    Walking between buildings and houses is somehting that I do constantly.

    It's a perfect way to relax in the end of the day and organize all my thoughts after a long day.

    It also the best way to know new places when I'm travelling.

  • Walking in the woods

    Sometimes, mainly when I'm feeling stressed, hiking in the woods is the best way to relax.

    This is also a chance for appreciating some nature.

    But also a little dangerous depdending on the day time.

  • Walking in the mountains

    I can't say this is the best kind of walk (IT IS!), but I always have the best experiences when I do them.

    I love the feeling of you seeing everything from the top of a mountain.
